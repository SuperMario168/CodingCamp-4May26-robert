# Product

**Todo Life Dashboard** — a mini project built during CodingCamp batch 04-05-2026 (started May 4, 2026).

The product is a personal productivity dashboard centered around todo/task management. The exact feature set is being defined via the spec at `.kiro/specs/todo-life-dashboard/`.

## Goals
- Help users manage daily tasks and life activities in one place
- Provide a clear, actionable dashboard view of todos and progress
